We are thrilled that you are considering Zoe Life Academy for your child’s early education journey! At our preschool, we believe in the word of God as our guide in creating a nurturing and engaging environment where every child can thrive and discover their unique potential.

At Zoe Life Academy for Kidz (ZLA), we understand that choosing the right preschool is a significant decision. Our dedicated team is here to support you and your family every step of the way, answer any questions you may have, and ensure you feel confident in your choice. Thank you for considering ZLA!

Our Service To You

  • Full and half-day care for children from 3 months to 6 years (Grade R)
  • Freshly cooked healthy meals prepared daily
  • A child-friendly environment that feels like a home away from home
  • A Christian approach where children are taught bible verses, prayer, and worship
  • Developmentally appropriate educational programs and stimulation
  • Professional support from Occupational and Speech therapy
  • Optional Swimming (R1500/term) and Karate (R250/month) classes
  • We are open during school holidays to support working families

Application Process

Our process is simple. The first step is to fill out our online application form. Once submitted, we will contact you to schedule a tour and finalize the next steps.

Required Documents

  • Completed Enrolment Form
  • Copy of Child’s Birth Certificate
  • ID Copies of Parents/Guardians
  • ID copy of designated person (if parents are foreigners)
  • Proof of Residence
  • Medical History Form (therapy reports etc.)
  • Report from current school (if applicable)
  • Immunization Records
