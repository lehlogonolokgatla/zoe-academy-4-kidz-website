Parent Resources

Our Weekly Menu

We are proud to provide freshly cooked, healthy meals for our children every day. Here is a sample of our menu variation.

Breakfast

Brown Porridge, White Porridge, and Oats. (No milk is added to accommodate children who are lactose intolerant).

Lunch

A rotation of hearty meals including Chicken & Pap, Mince & Spaghetti, Canned Fish & Rice, and Sausage & Pap. Lunches are served with vegetables like mixed veg, cabbage, or spinach.

Snacks

We provide a morning and afternoon snack, which includes a sandwich and juice/tea, as well as seasonal fruit.

Extracurricular Activities

We partner with external suppliers to offer exciting optional activities. Fees for these activities are paid directly to the suppliers.

Prices: Karate — R250 / month • Swimming — R1500 / term
