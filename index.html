Welcome to Zoe Life Academy for Kidz

Nurturing Every Child's Unique Journey

A safe and loving preschool where neurodivergent and mainstream learners are empowered to learn and grow, guided by the principle to "Train a child God's way."

Schedule a Tour

Faith-Based Foundation

Our curriculum is rooted in gentle, faith-based principles, creating a loving and compassionate atmosphere where every child feels valued.

Neurodiversity-Affirming

We celebrate the unique strengths of each child, using individualized strategies to support children with autism, ADHD, and other learning differences.

Holistic Development

We focus on the whole child, nurturing their spiritual, emotional, social, and academic growth in a supportive, play-based environment.

Mainstream Learning

We provide a robust academic foundation that prepares children for a seamless transition into mainstream primary education,ensuring every child develops the confidence and skills to thrive in any classroom setting.

A Place to Thrive

From sensory exploration and creative arts to early literacy and numeracy, our programs are designed to meet your child where they are. We foster a love for learning in a structured yet flexible setting.
